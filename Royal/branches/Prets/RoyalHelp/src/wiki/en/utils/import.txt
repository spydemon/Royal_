Import BirDy 0.2.0 database
=====

In this window, you can choose your language and your database.

Choose your language
....................
* Click on the "Language" tab and chosse your language. A reboot is necessery to save the changes.

Choose your database
....................
* Click on the "Database" tab and choose your databade. After saving, the changing is immediate.
* You also can create a new database or edit one of them.
