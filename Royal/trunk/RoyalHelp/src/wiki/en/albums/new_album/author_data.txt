Author Information
==================

This window allow you to link authors to the new album. If an author doesn't exist, he can be created through this window

$wizard.img$

Nickname
........

* Author's nickname.
* This name has to be unique.
* This field is compulsory

Name
....

* Author's name.
* This name has to be unique.


Surname
.......

* Author's surname.

Dates de Naissance et Décès
...........................

* Author's birth date and death date.
* Click on the field to make a calendar appear, and select the date.

$calendar.img$

* If the two fields are fill, make sure that the birth date is lower than the death date
